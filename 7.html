7.1 Discrete energy and radioactivity

Energy level

  • different orbits have different amounts of energy

The energy level of hydrogen
  • the energy level has a discrete or quantised energy
  • excited state: electron occupying an energy level higher than the ground state(n=1)
  • electrons cannot have a value between -13.58 and -3.39 -> it has quantised energy
